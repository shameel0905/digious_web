## Reporting a Vulnerability

## Security Vulnerability Reporting Guidelines  

**Purpose:**  
The purpose of these guidelines is to ensure that security vulnerabilities in the repository are reported in a responsible manner, allowing Digious Solutions to assess, address, and mitigate issues effectively and efficiently.  

### 1. **Identify Issue**  
Before reporting, make sure what you have found is a genuine security vulnerability. Conduct preliminary analysis to understand whether it's a real threat or expected functionality of the application.  

### 2. **Collect Information**  
Gather all relevant information regarding the vulnerability without exploiting it. Information may include:  
- Description of the vulnerability  
- Steps to reproduce the issue  
- Possible implications of the vulnerability  
- How the issue can be resolved, if known  
- Any other relevant information  

### 3. **Prepare Your Report**  
Prepare a clear, concise, and detailed report. Include:  
- Contact information (in case further communication or clarification is required)  
- Detailed explanation of the vulnerability  
- Steps to reproduce (if applicable)  
- Relevant screenshots, logs, or code snippets  
- Potential impact and severity  

### 4. **Securely Send Your Email**  
- Email your findings to **devolution@digioussolutions.com**.  
- Use a descriptive subject line: e.g., "Security Vulnerability Report - [Short Description of the Issue]".  
- Encrypt sensitive details using PGP/GPG if possible. If encryption is not available, avoid sending sensitive details that might compromise the system further.  

### 5. **Do Not Disclose the Issue Publicly**  
Do not share or disclose the details of the security vulnerability in public forums, social media, or other public channels until it has been resolved and disclosed appropriately by Digious Solutions.  

### 6. **Wait for Response**  
- Allow reasonable time for the team to investigate and respond to your report. This can typically take up to 30 days.  
- Digious Solutions should acknowledge receipt of your report within a reasonable timeframe, provide regular updates, and inform you once the issue has been resolved.  

### 7. **Feedback on Resolution**  
Once the vulnerability has been resolved, you may be asked for feedback on the resolution and reporting process. This helps improve the security practices for future incident handling.  

### 8. **Confidentiality Agreement**  
Upon reporting, you agree to keep information about the security vulnerability confidential between you and Digious Solutions until an authorized disclosure is made. This is to prevent exploitation by malicious actors and ensure that users are not put at unnecessary risk.  

---  
This framework ensures a professional and systematic approach to managing security vulnerabilities, safeguarding both the infrastructure and the users of Digious Solutions.
